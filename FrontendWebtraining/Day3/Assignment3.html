My Favorite Book: Rich Dad Poor Dad

About The Book

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" is a bestselling personal finance book written by Robert T. Kiyosaki, first published in 1997. It has become one of the most popular financial books of all time, known for its simple yet powerful lessons about money, wealth, and investing.
